How is it working ?

The extension is first looking at your history and check if a website of scans is known. So it work well if you active synchronisation between your different devices !

Then if the website is known, the extension tries to get the chapter and title of the scan you watched by recognizing pattern in the title. If the pattern is recognized, the scan is saved.

So if your scan is not saved, it means the website can be unknown or the pattern is wrong. In both case, feel free to contact me so that i can fix it or tell you why i can't !

Some website are difficult to handle, because the pattern could change for each scan for the title of the webpage or because the title is totally wrong sometimes.
